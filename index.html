In Progress

Long Range Custom Drone

I am currently developing an experimental micro-category drone specifically focused on pushing the boundaries of flight endurance and signal range, with the ultimate goal of evolving it into a fully autonomous surveillance UAV. Utilizing a Pixhawk flight controller to leverage robust GPS automation features, this phase of testing explores the integration of high-energy-density Lithium-Ion (Li-Ion) battery packs and low-KV propulsion setups to maximize battery life, alongside low-frequency 900MHz telemetry links to extend operational range. By refining these core capabilities through active experimentation, the project aims to establish a reliable long-range hardware foundation capable of eventually supporting high-definition camera payloads, automated grid patrols, and persistent aerial monitoring missions.
